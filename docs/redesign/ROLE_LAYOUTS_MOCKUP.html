Summary of the pattern

It's not tabs — tabs would add a click for the role that least needs one. The rule is: Check-ins is a sidebar page only where the backlog needs triage. Mechanics triage (multiple categories, passbacks, varied urgency). MOT testers don't (one category, simple queue). Managers get the sidebar page plus an embedded summary in Today for the daily glance.

My Work is the same for all three roles — a personal feed of "jobs assigned to me" — so it's not role-specific in shape, just in content.
